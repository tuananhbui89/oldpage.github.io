Selected Publications

Generative Adversarial AutoEncoder

Overcome the mode collapse problem of GAN by using the regurlarization the relative distance between data samples and the latent samples that encoded from data samples by autoencoder. We expect if two samples is near in data space will also near in latent space and so on.
Ngoc-Trung Tran, Tuan-Anh Bui, Ngai-Man Cheung
Under review ECCV,2018
PDF Project

On-device Scalable Image-based Localization

We present the scalable design of an entire on-device system for large-scale urban localization. The proposed design integrates compact image retrieval and 2D-3D correspondence search to estimate the camera pose in a city region of extensive coverage.
Ngoc-Trung Tran, Dang-Khoa Le Tan, Anh-Dzung Doan, Thanh-Toan Do, Tuan-Anh Bui, Ngai-Man Cheung
Under review TIP,2018
PDF Project

SceneNet: A generative model for generating scene from 6-DOF camera pose

Given a set of images of a space, such as an office, which is captured by several cameras, how can infer a scene of that space when viewed from a particular point of view? In this project, I assume that it’s a relationship between the scenes and the viewpoints of these scenes, that can learned by a GAN.
Tuan-Anh Bui
