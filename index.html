Dist-GAN: Improving GAN by distance constraints

Overcome the mode collapse problem of GAN by using the regurlarization the relative distance between data samples and the latent samples that encoded from data samples by autoencoder. We expect if two samples is near in data space will also near in latent space and so on.

On-device Scalable Image-based Localization via Prioritized Cascade Search and Fast One-Many RANSAC

We present the scalable design of an entire on-device system for large-scale urban localization. The proposed design integrates compact image retrieval and 2D-3D correspondence search to estimate the camera pose in a city region of extensive coverage.
